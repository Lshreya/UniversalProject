INNOVATIVE ACCESS SOLUTIONS

For over 35 years, Universal Scaffolding & Equipment has built an impressive reputation for its dedication to quality and customer service. Our engineering staff is committed to the continuous improvement of product design and functionality.

Our innovative products are produced utilizing state-of-the-art manufacturing techniques and materials. Universal’s Total Quality Management system ensures we manufacture consistently high quality products. Our commitment to customer service is unmatched - our customers come first. We take a hands-on approach to customer service and our knowledgeable sales staff and product engineers are always available to answer general inquiries and address technical issues

Universal’s centrally located warehouse and distribution enables our customers to receive shipments quickly and efficiently. We maintain one of the largest inventories of scaffolding and shoring and forming products in the United States, with a diverse product line to meet all customer needs.
